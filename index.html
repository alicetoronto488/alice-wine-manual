Wine Fundamentals

At Alice, wine isn't just a beverage — it's part of the experience. We are an upscale casual steakhouse and late-night dining destination in the heart of Little Italy. Our guests expect guidance, and the staff member who confidently leads them through the wine list earns trust, bigger tabs, and better tips. Here's your foundation.

The Five Families

Sparkling — Think celebration, effervescence, first impressions. Offer it the moment guests sit down, especially for special occasions. Champagne and Prosecco open wallets and set the tone for the entire meal.

White — Crisp, refreshing, versatile. Ranges from bone-dry Sauvignon Blanc to buttery Chardonnay. Whites shine with our seafood dishes, pastas, and lighter appetizers. Always serve chilled.

Rosé — The patio wine. Beautiful colour, approachable flavour. Rosé thrives in the warm months and pairs effortlessly with Alice's shareables and salads. Don't underestimate it — rosé bottles move fast on the right night.

Orange Wine — A conversation starter. White grapes made like red wine — skin contact gives it that amber hue and tannic grip. We carry Poderi Cellario, and it's a secret weapon for adventurous guests and rich appetizers.

Red — The backbone of our list and the soul of a steakhouse. From smooth Merlots to bold Cabernets and our flagship Super Tuscan, reds pair with our Tomahawks, Striploins, and heartier mains. This is where the big upsell opportunities live.

Key Vocabulary You Need

Dry vs. Sweet — Most of our wines are dry (low residual sugar). The only exception with sweetness is the Moselland Riesling, which is off-dry. Know this so you can steer guests toward their preference.

Body — Light body (like our Pinot Grigio) feels like skim milk in the mouth. Full body (like Caymus Cabernet) feels like whole cream. Match heavy dishes with full-bodied wines, lighter plates with lighter wines.

Tannins — That drying, gripping sensation in bold reds. Tannins are softened by fat and protein — which is exactly why a tannic Cabernet is magical with our 28oz Tomahawk.
